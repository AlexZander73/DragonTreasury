Treasure replacement guide:

Provide optional sprites by type and rarity naming pattern:
- coin-common.png
- gem-rare.png
- artifact-epic.png
- legendary-relic-legendary.png
- cursed-item-rare.png
- metal-idol-epic.png
- arcane-crystal-rare.png
- scroll-capsule-common.png

Recommended size buckets:
small: 64x64
medium: 96x96
large: 128x128
huge: 160x160

Current implementation uses vector fallback primitives in src/scene/TreasureVisuals.ts.
