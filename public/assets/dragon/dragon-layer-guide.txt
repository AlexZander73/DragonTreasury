Dragon layered asset replacement guide:

Expected optional files:
- dragon-body.png
- dragon-head.png
- dragon-tail.png
- dragon-wing.png
- dragon-eye.png
- dragon-glow.png

All should be transparent PNG/WebP sized relative to a 512x512 anchor.
Pivot assumptions in code:
- body center: (0, 0)
- head offset: (+82, -72)
- tail offset: (-120, +30)
- wing offset: (-26, -46)

If sprite sheets are preferred later, add metadata and switch draw logic in src/scene/DragonActor.ts.
